About

Welcome to our women's and men's clothing ecommerce website! Let’s share the story behind this exciting portfolio project for Holberton School from Lille.

Inspired by our passion for tech and design, we created this ecommerce site using HTML, CSS and JavaScript for the frontend, Python with Django for the backend and MySQL for the database. A friend who owns a boutique asked us to help expand her business online, sparking our enthusiasm for the project.

We focused on her vision, studying her collection and values. Our goal was to create an ecommerce site that reflects her store's aesthetics and provides a seamless online shopping experience.

Explore our website, showcasing our dedication and creativity as Holberton School students. Discover our impressive portfolio project!
